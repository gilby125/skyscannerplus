Skyscanner+

Looking for an awesome destination? Just let us know what you want

Learn more about us

Skyscanner Plus is an extension for the metasearch engine Skyscanner, that enables people to use a natural processing language to compare flights. The customer start a conversation with the bot by telling the departure airport and the destination. After a short interaction between the bot and the client, you can complete the booking process on Skyscanner. It is free for all Skyscanner users.
